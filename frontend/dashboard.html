How Credits are Calculated:

• Monthly Limit: 10,000 kg CO₂ (10 Kilotonnes)

• Daily Limit: 333.33 kg CO₂ (Monthly limit ÷ 30 days)

• Credit Calculation: 1 Carbon Credit = 1 kg CO₂ reduced

• If emissions < daily limit: Credits Earned = (Daily Limit - Actual Emissions)

• If emissions > daily limit: Credits Deficit = (Actual Emissions - Daily Limit)
